🎯 Design Overview

11 core screens for the Verified Vibe MVP, featuring:

  • 4 Archetypes: Casual Man, Spoilt Woman, Marriage-Minded Man, Safety-First Woman
  • Trust Verification: Government ID, Bank statements, 5+ photos, Q&A responses
  • Trust Score: 0-100 based on Identity (35%), Lifestyle (45%), Intent (20%)
  • Anti-Swipe Design: Daily pass limits, archetype-filtered feed, mutual match required

🎭 The 4 Launch Archetypes

🎯

Casual Man

Wants casual dating leading to consensual connection. No games, real vibes.

Matches with: Spoilt Woman (primary), Marriage-Minded (secondary)

💎

Spoilt Woman

Wants to be wined, dined, and treated well. Expects quality and attention.

Matches with: Casual Man (primary), Marriage-Minded (secondary)

💍

Marriage-Minded Man

Wants long-term partner, family, stability. Serious intentions.

Matches with: Safety-First Woman (primary), Committed (secondary)

🛡️

Safety-First Woman

Won't compromise on safety. Wants verified non-creep, transparent intentions.

Matches with: Marriage-Minded Man (primary), Trustworthy (secondary)
