# Personality Profile — Dante
**Archetype:** The BDSM-Lifestyle / Dominant Man
**Age:** 35 | **Occupation:** Structural Engineer / Event Organiser (lifestyle events)
**Platform:** FetLife, Feeld

---

## Who He Is
Dante has been in the BDSM community for eight years and has standing within it. He organises events, has mentored people newer to the lifestyle, and runs his dominance in a way that is practised rather than intuitive — which makes it safer and, for the right person, more consistent than naturally dominant men who have never examined what they're doing. His dominance is not his entire personality; he is warm, self-deprecating, and surprisingly funny outside a dynamic. He understands the difference between being a Dom and being a dominant man in general — and he doesn't bleed one into the other without consent.

---

## What He's Looking For
- Stated: A submissive partner who engages the lifestyle seriously and knows herself within a dynamic
- Actual: A connection where the D/s dimension is part of a real relationship — not just a scene partner
- He is more romantically invested than he initially presents; the structure is his entry point, not his limit

---

## Green Flags
- Community-verified: references exist, people vouch for him, he has standing
- Negotiates before every new dynamic, every new scene — it is a habit, not a ritual
- Aftercare is specific to the person, not a default script
- Can leave the dynamic at the door when she needs him to be a human being
- Has said no to scenes he had the power to push through — the limits go both ways

---

## Yellow Flags
- The Dom frame is so practised it can feel like a persona rather than a person
- Occasional difficulty with reciprocity outside of a dynamic — listening without leading
- His community standing can produce a mild sense of untouchability
- Previous connections who left the lifestyle while with him — pattern of diverging needs
- Has strong opinions about how D/s "should" work; slow to adjust for individual variation

---

## Red Flags
- Has violated negotiated limits and explained it as intuition or "reading the moment"
- Uses the power dynamic outside agreed parameters — controlling her life outside scenes
- Dismisses safe words or limit-setting as "inexperience" or "resistance to growth"
- His community references are all people he trained or is currently involved with
- Aftercare disappears when the scene ends — emotional drop handled alone

---

## Compatibility Pattern
Dante is compatible with experienced submissives who have a clear sense of their own limits and enough community standing to verify his. The dynamic works when both people are practised, honest, and can step outside the structure to be emotionally present with each other. It fails when the D/s frame substitutes for the relationship rather than existing inside it.

---

## How He Shows Up in an Interview
- Calm and deliberate — interviews like he runs a negotiation
- Ask him to describe a scene that went somewhere unexpected and how he handled it
- Ask what a relationship with him looks like outside of a dynamic
- Ask whether he has ever stopped a scene against a partner's protest and whether he was right to
- Best question: "What has submission taught you about yourself?" — the direction of the insight matters
