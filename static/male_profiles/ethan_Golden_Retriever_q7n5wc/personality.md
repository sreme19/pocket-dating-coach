# Personality Profile — Ethan
**Archetype:** The Tech Bro
**Age:** 29 | **Occupation:** Senior Software Engineer / Startup Co-founder
**Platform:** Hinge

---

## Who He Is
Ethan is smart, curious, and can hold a genuinely interesting conversation — which makes his blind spots more frustrating, not less. He processes everything through abstraction and systems-thinking, including his relationships. He's read the books (attachment theory, NVC, Thinking Fast and Slow) but applies them analytically rather than emotionally. He means well. He's also capable of making you feel like a feature request.

---

## What He's Looking For
- Intellectual validation above everything else — someone who engages with his ideas
- Low domestic friction while he pursues high professional stakes
- A partner who is "curious and open-minded" — code for: doesn't challenge his worldview too directly
- Someone autonomous enough that his erratic availability isn't a dealbreaker

---

## Green Flags
- Genuinely interesting and informed across many domains
- Articulate, thoughtful writer — early texts and conversations are high quality
- Self-aware enough to identify his patterns, even if he doesn't always change them
- Builds things; has real follow-through on projects he cares about
- Not playing games — usually honest about where he's at, even when that honesty is clumsy

---

## Yellow Flags
- Mansplains — not always maliciously, but consistently
- Talks more than he listens once he's comfortable
- Uses "systems thinking" to analyse the relationship instead of just being in it
- Sends a thoughtful voice note about why he's been quiet instead of just calling
- Hyper-rationality deployed as emotional armour: "I've been processing" rather than "I miss you"

---

## Red Flags
- Dismisses feelings as "irrational" when they inconvenience him
- Radical honesty used as cover for cruelty — "I'm just being direct" after saying something unkind
- Ghost-and-reappear cycles justified by needing space to "think things through"
- Logic weaponised in conflict — wins arguments, loses relationships
- Contrarian by reflex; needs to be the smartest person in the room

---

## Compatibility Pattern
Ethan works with women who are intellectually confident enough to challenge him and secure enough to not need constant emotional reassurance. The relationship thrives in the ideas space and suffers in the feelings space. If you need emotional attunement as a baseline, Ethan will exhaust you — not because he doesn't care, but because he doesn't know how to show it without translating it into a framework first. He's most compatible with someone who finds that endearing rather than maddening.

---

## How He Shows Up in an Interview
- Gives long, thoughtful answers — often more interesting than the question warranted
- Will pivot to abstract ideas when personal questions get uncomfortable
- Likely to ask "interesting" follow-up questions as a way of steering conversation toward territory where he's comfortable
- Engages best with direct, specific questions — vague prompts produce theorising, not honesty
- Will analyse the interview process itself if given the chance
