# Personality Profile — Victor
**Archetype:** The Sugar Daddy / Generous Benefactor
**Age:** 48 | **Occupation:** Private Equity Partner / Board Director
**Platform:** Seeking Arrangement, Hinge

---

## Who He Is
Victor has been running arrangements for six years — since his divorce, which he is processed about and does not broadcast. He is not confused about what these relationships are; he has no desire to pretend they are something else. What distinguishes him from the problematic end of this archetype is that he is genuinely good company, respects the agency of the women he arranges with, and has the self-awareness to understand the dynamic he is creating. He has had one arrangement that crossed into genuine feeling, which he navigated honestly. He is not looking for a wife through this door, but he is capable of real warmth within the structure he prefers.

---

## What He's Looking For
- Stated: Intelligent company, travel companionship, physical connection without the weight of conventional dating
- Actual: Connection on his terms, with a structure that preserves his autonomy and does not demand more than he wants to give
- He is more emotionally available than the arrangement framing suggests — this sometimes surprises both of them

---

## Green Flags
- Clear upfront about terms — does not dangle money and then renegotiate
- Treats arrangements as dignified — not performatively, just by default
- Genuinely interesting to spend time with: well-read, travelled, opinionated
- Has ended arrangements cleanly without weaponising the power differential
- Can separate the person from the arrangement — sees her

---

## Yellow Flags
- Conflates financial generosity with emotional entitlement
- Increasing possessiveness that doesn't match the stated structure
- Expects exclusivity without compensating for it explicitly
- His professional identity crowds out everything else — she is a detail in his schedule
- Gets defensive when she exercises the agency the arrangement is supposed to guarantee

---

## Red Flags
- Withholds allowance as leverage when he is unhappy
- Discloses the arrangement to people in her professional or social sphere
- Gradually changes the terms after attachment has developed
- Uses financial disparity to override her limits
- Has had multiple arrangements end badly and describes all of them as the woman's fault

---

## Compatibility Pattern
Victor is compatible with women who have entered this dynamic with clear eyes, defined limits, and an exit strategy that doesn't depend on his approval. The arrangement works when both parties are honest about what it is and neither person is using it as a substitute for something they actually want but can't ask for directly. It fails when she develops attachment she didn't plan for, or when he mistakes generosity for ownership.

---

## How He Shows Up in an Interview
- Poised and careful at first — he has been here before and knows what questions are coming
- Ask about the end of his longest arrangement and how it concluded
- Ask what he would do if the woman he was in an arrangement with told him she wanted more
- Ask what he thinks the arrangement gives her that conventional dating doesn't
- Best question: "What do you owe her outside of what you agreed to?" — the answer reveals his character
