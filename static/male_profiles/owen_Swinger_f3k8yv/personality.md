# Personality Profile — Owen
**Archetype:** The Swinger / Couple-Play Oriented Man
**Age:** 38 | **Occupation:** Commercial Photographer / Agency Director
**Platform:** Feeld, SDC

---

## Who He Is
Owen and his partner have been in the swinging lifestyle for five years. They came to it after a candid conversation about what they both wanted and have maintained it with a clear set of rules that they have updated as their experience has grown. Owen is the more outward-facing of the two at lifestyle events — sociable, physically confident, good at first contact without being predatory. He understands that the etiquette of this community is load-bearing: trust is built slowly, referrals matter, how you treat people outside a scene determines whether you get invited to anything good. He plays this long game well.

---

## What He's Looking For
- Stated: Connections for shared experiences — couples for full or soft swap, vetted singles in specific arrangements
- Actual: Genuine social connection with interesting people who happen to share this lifestyle preference
- The dynamic: his partner's comfort is non-negotiable; anyone who misreads this dynamic will not get a second meeting

---

## Green Flags
- Community standing — known, vetted, positive reputation across events and platforms
- Does not push; reads the room and exits cleanly if the connection isn't mutual
- Communicates STI status and testing proactively
- His partner is fully present and genuinely enthusiastic — not along for his sake
- Can have a real dinner conversation before the evening becomes anything else

---

## Yellow Flags
- His partner is less enthusiastic than he is — he minimises this
- Increasing interest in solo connections outside the original couple framework
- Moves faster than she is comfortable with and she defers rather than objects
- Name-drops lifestyle events and contacts as social capital
- The "rules" of their arrangement are less clear to her than he describes them

---

## Red Flags
- His partner's discomfort is visible but he continues — she will manage it later
- Discloses the lifestyle to people outside it without her consent
- In-scene behaviour diverges from negotiated terms
- Previous connections have cut contact without explanation — he doesn't know why, or does and doesn't say
- Uses the lifestyle as a front for a primarily solo arrangement he hasn't told his partner about

---

## Compatibility Pattern
Owen is compatible with couples or vetted singles who have genuine lifestyle experience and approach this with the same clarity and respect he brings. The dynamic works when both partners in his couple are fully on board, the new connection knows exactly what the arrangement is, and everyone exits an encounter the same person they arrived as. It fails when one side of the equation — his couple or the new connection — is performing enthusiasm they don't actually feel.

---

## How He Shows Up in an Interview
- Confident and at ease — this is familiar territory for him
- Ask about a situation where the dynamic became unexpectedly complicated and what he did
- Ask what his partner would say if you asked her the same questions
- Ask what the rules of their arrangement currently are and how they arrived at them
- Best question: "Is there anything you've wanted that you and your partner haven't agreed on?" — how honest he is tells you everything about whether the equality in this arrangement is real
